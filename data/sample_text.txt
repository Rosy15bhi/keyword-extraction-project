Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming modern business environments.
Companies are increasingly using machine learning algorithms to analyze large amounts of data and support strategic decision making.
In marketing, data-driven approaches allow organizations to better understand customer behavior and personalize user experiences.
At the same time, automation technologies improve operational efficiency and reduce human error.
Keyword extraction techniques play an important role in identifying the most relevant concepts within large textual datasets, helping analysts to quickly gain insights and make informed decisions.
